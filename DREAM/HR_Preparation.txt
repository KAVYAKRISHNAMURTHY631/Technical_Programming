HR INTERVIEW PREPARATION
=======================

1. Tell me about yourself
   - Background
   - Skills
   - Projects
   - Career goals

2. Strengths
   - Problem-solving
   - Consistency
   - Willingness to learn

3. Weaknesses
   - Overthinking (working on it)
   - Perfectionism

4. Why should we hire you?
   - Strong fundamentals
   - Continuous learner
   - Good team player

5. Why FAANG / Product-based company?
   - Learning culture
   - Challenging problems
   - Growth opportunities

6. Handling pressure
   - Break problem into steps
   - Stay calm
   - Ask clarifying questions

7. Failure & Learning
   - Explain past rejection
   - Lessons learned
   - How you improved

8. Career Goals
   - Short term: Strong software engineer
   - Long term: Tech lead / Architect
